Take control over the display of variation prices in your store.

== Description ==

The WooCommerce Variation Prices plugin lets you take control over the display of variation prices in your store.

Main features:
 * Works everywhere
 * Replaces the variation price range on the single product page
 * Appears on the WooCommerce shop and category pages
 * Works with related products, other plugins (e.g. Product Table and Quick View), and more

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin from the Order Confirmation page or using the link in your order email
2. Go to Plugins -> Add New -> Upload and select the ZIP file you downloaded
3. Once installed, click to Activate the plugin.
4. Enter your license key under WooCommerce -> Settings -> Products -> Variation Prices
5. See the [documentation](https://barn2.com/kb-categories/wvp-kb/) for further details.
